About Me

Hi there! My name is Scott (Scooter)! I’ve been repairing computers for 2 years now, and have refurbished about 200 in total. I’ve also been building and fixing gaming PCs for about a year. I’ve put countless hours into understanding all of the data and issues behind each part of the computer, so you can assure you’re getting top-quality work if you buy from me!

Because I go to school at Miami University, I’ve been volunteering with HYPE Computer Refurbishment to fix Miami’s retired computers & projectors and donate them to underfunded schools. I’ve led 2 donations to Africa, one esports donation to Melvindale Esports here in Michigan, and been a part of 12 other projects. I enjoy working on computers for the joy of helping people (and the challenge of fixing weird issues).

I ultimately hope to grow this into a full-scale business one day, and this small service-area business is how I’m starting it all!
